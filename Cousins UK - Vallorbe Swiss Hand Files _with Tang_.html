Vallorbe Swiss Hand Files "with Tang"

Vallorbe Swiss
  • Superlative Swiss Vallorbe manufacture.
  • The quality is unrivalled and the range vast.


Selection is only a matter of deciding on type, length-size and cut. Please refer to the few guidelines below to ensure correct ordering.

As a rule the description boxes will show dimensions in the order of length, cut and section (width x height).

Overall lengths or cutting lengths (without the tang/handle) are distinguished by letters OL or CL.

Cuts expressed in numbers, simply get finer as the number rises.
Eg: 0=Coarse, 2=Medium, 5=Fine etc.
Dotted outer profiles indicate cutting faces.
